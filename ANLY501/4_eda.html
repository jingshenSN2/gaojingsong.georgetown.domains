Exploring Data

1. PC Component Database

All data of amazon products are stored in SQLite database with multiple tables.

cpu, gpu, drive tables store the product information with ASIN as a primary key. cpu_ts, gpu_ts, drive_ts tables store the product historical data with (ASIN, keepa_minute, keepa_idx) as a primary key. keepa_idx refers to different historical data such as price, sales volume, etc.

2.1 Time Series of Chia

The cleaned historical data of chia coin is visualized by ggplot in R.

From the plots, there is a short period of "chia fever" in May 2021 with the price hit a peak at 1550 USD and the market capacity hit a peak at 570 million USD. Meanwhile, the net space(total drive space used to mine chia) increased dramatically from 0.01 EiB to more than 10 EiB.

But in June, though the net space continued to raise from 10 EiB to 30 EiB, the price and market capacity decreased a lot to 300 USD/coin and 300 million USD. After July, the market of chia cooled down. The price, market capacity, and netspace maintained at a relatively stable level since then.
