2.4 Summary of Associated Rule Mining

Many underlying relationships between words in the YouTube comments dataset are found by the associate rule mining, which gives a more complete view of the dataset. But some relationships indicate that the keywords and categories used in auto-labeling procedure in previous section are inaccurate. For example, some keywords have low support and should not be used as labeling standard. Also, a host/channel-related category is needed because many comments are about thar topic.
